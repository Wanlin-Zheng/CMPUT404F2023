Question 1: What are some of the benefits of using a static site generator? What are some disadvantages?

Theres no dynamic functionality and less security risks as you're just presenting information. Static webpages can be more of a hassle to update. Using a generator allows for easier template creation and it may already be organized for you. However you might have less creative range than creating a website from scratch.
